About

We are a group of automotive enthusiasts working diligently on an invariable endeavor to build an All-terrain vehicle that can take up the challenges of hard terrain with ease, along with security and comfort for the driver. Learning new concepts and improving upon previous mistakes is a part and parcel of this entire breathtaking process and it all boils down to one week of rigorous competition where hundreds of teams from across the country and abroad compete with their vehicles to prove their mettle and the standards of the ATV that they fabricate themselves. The event by the tagline of “SAE BAJA INDIA” witnesses a number of challenging events with tests such as Brake Test, Suspension test, Hill Climb test, Maneuverability, etc. The ATV is first tested for its response to various impacts and stresses, economy of design and material used, in the virtual round and then tested on field to its limits.

Team

Our team consists of 25 members, each one of them working with a common goal of transforming a thought into reality with utmost accuracy, while inculcating the engineering concepts we learn at college level and beyond. The ATV we fabricated last year had a top speed of 55kmph, the weight of the vehicle being close to 208kgs, has a 10hp, 305cc Briggs & Stratton engine and has been designed for one passenger keeping in mind his security and comfort..

Achievements

Listing our achievements, we have a track record of successfully clearing the rigorous Technical Inspection every year since our debut in 2014. Our performance in the Virtual round of SAE BAJA INDIA was best among all the participating IITs in 2015, 13th position among 400 teams and at par performance with the best teams in Hill Climb event. We are improving every year in terms of our design and performance and with the present dedication that the entire team shares, we are confident towards a fruitful participation in the coming years.
