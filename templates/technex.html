About

Every year the Science and Technology Council, IIT (BHU) Gymkhana organises its annual techno-management fest, known far and wide as Technex. Started 77 years back as a carnival to celebrate technology and innovation, it provides a creative platform to the technical fanatics all over India to help them come over with innovative and out of the box ideas. It offers a plethora of events which help the technically enthusiastic crowd of various colleges to explore their prowess in their chosen technical field.

This technical extravaganza along with promoting technical activities among the students also works on bringing social awareness in the society with the help of innovative social initiatives. Technex ‘16 organized Green India Clean India Campaign under which plantation and cleanliness drives were organized. There was a celebration of Green Week which concluded with Recycle Fair. The 2017 edition would mark the 78th iteration of the fest.

Technex '17 will be conducted from 24th to 26th February, 2017. Learn more about Technex at www.technex.in.

Events

The main goal of Technex is to make the students of IIT (BHU) aware of the recent technological advancements going on in the institute as well as outside. Technex aims at inculcating the habit of “building something” within the students. They enjoy the process of creation and use it as a tool to make our society a better place to live in through various task-based and project-based events. Technex has a perfect balance between hardware and software related events giving students equal exposure to both. The events see equal representation of all the departments as they inculcate every discipline in the problem statements of the events.

Think Talks

Technex organizes Guest Lectures of various eminent speakers from varied fields of science and technology under the banner “THINK TALKS”. The following speakers graced Technex '16 and encouraged students to pursue their interests in various technical fields.

  • Dr. Rosaly Lopes, Senior Research Scientist, NASA
  • Ms. Vinita Bali, MD and CEO, Britannia
  • Mr. Gianni Di Caro, Senior Researcher - IDSIA, Switzerland
  • Mr. Atul Gurtu, Lead Scientist - Hadron Collider, CERN
  • Mr. Santosh Desai, MD and CEO, FutureBrands
  • Mr. Prabhu Chawla, Editorial Director, The New Indian Express

Exhibitions

Many companies and researchers exhibit their advanced products hailing from various areas of science and engineering in Technex. These serve as a great source of knowledge for the participants as they get to know a lot about what new things are being devised to make the life of humans easier. Technex '16 witnessed the following exhibitions.

  • Air Show
  • Manav - India’s First Humanoid Robot
  • Virtual and Augmented Reality
  • Swarm Robotics
  • Israeli Innovator

Corporate Conclave

Technex ‘16 presented the first ever edition of Corporate Conclave, which brought together the biggest corporate stalwarts of present times. The conclave aims to establish a platform through which our students can interact with executives, to prepare themselves for the corporate battle ahead. The conclave consisted of interactive workshops and discussions between company executives and students, to help the latter foster a better knowledge and awareness of the know-how of the industry.

Startup Fair

Startup Fair is one of the biggest national convention for young entrepreneurs being organized by IIT (BHU) Varanasi in association with MCIIE. It features a plethora of events including Guest Lectures, Elevator Pitch, Workshops, Networking Meet and Panel Discussions with a goal is to give something to the startup community by introducing emerging startups to resources, mentorship, and investment opportunities.
